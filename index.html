Expert Reccomnedations and Purchasings

If you share the passion for the outdoors and for biking like we do, let us do the logitical part. No one likes having to sit through dozens of websites and look for the right fit for your bike at the right price. Let us do your shopping for you. Let us know what you need and we will contact our local supply chains to get it for you faster and cheaper than anywhere else.

Above and Beyond Traditional Repair

As the name suggests, at Big Boss we go big or go home. Our philosophy of never settling for mediocraty is why we offer repairs and tuning like no one else. Our experts don't just fix and tune your bike with standard tools. No no, we use our own custom tuning and analysis software to find the true breadth of damage your bike may have sustained and to find exactly where your bike needs tuning and where it can do without.

Ultimate Bike Security

We like to set ourselves apart from the competition as much as possible. We strive to provide you with not only a sense of security knowing your bike has been fixed and tuned to absolute perfection, we want to provide your bike with physical security no matter where you are. With a single call to any of our locaitons, a "Lockdown" team will be sent to your location and deliver a top-of-the-line padlock for your bike (incase you forgot yours).
